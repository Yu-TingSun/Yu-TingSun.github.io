## 概述 | Overview

酶类蛋白catalyze DNA的复制、修复、重组、拓扑变化和化学修饰等核心代谢过程。这些酶通过精确识别DNA结构和序列，保证遗传信息的忠实传递和适时调控。

Enzymatic proteins catalyze core metabolic processes including DNA replication, repair, recombination, topology changes, and chemical modifications. These enzymes ensure faithful transmission and timely regulation of genetic information through precise recognition of DNA structure and sequence.
