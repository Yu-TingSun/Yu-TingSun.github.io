## Research Themes

My publications span fundamental biophysics to biological applications:

- **DNA-Protein Interactions**: RecA-mediated homologous recombination, eukaryotic recombinases (Dmc1, Hop2-Mnd1)
- **Biopolymer Physics**: DNA behavior under spatial constraints and confinement
- **Single-Molecule Biophysics**: FRET, CoSMoS, and microfluidic approaches
- **Biomimetic Systems**: Vesicle-DNA interactions and cell adhesion models
